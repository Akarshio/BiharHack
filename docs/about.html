About BiharHack

Connecting Bihar's Tech Community Through Events & Innovation

What is BiharHack?

BiharHack is a dedicated platform designed to bridge the gap between tech enthusiasts and technology events happening across Bihar. We understand that students and professionals often miss out on valuable opportunities like workshops, hackathons, meetups, and tech conferences simply because they don't know when and where these events are happening.

Our platform serves as a centralized hub where anyone can discover, explore, and stay updated about tech events in Bihar, making it easier for the community to connect, learn, and grow together.

Open Source Project

Why BiharHack?

Bihar's tech ecosystem is growing rapidly, but information about events is often scattered across different platforms, social media groups, and college notice boards. This makes it challenging for students to:

  • Stay updated about upcoming tech events in their city
  • Find relevant workshops and hackathons to enhance their skills
  • Network with speakers and fellow tech enthusiasts
  • Discover opportunities to showcase their talents

BiharHack solves this problem by providing a single platform where all tech events in Bihar are listed, making it effortless for students and professionals to find opportunities that match their interests.

Who Can Use BiharHack?

Students

College students can register on the platform, discover tech events happening near them, and even list events organized by their colleges or tech clubs. This helps create a collaborative ecosystem where students support each other's growth.

Speakers & Industry Professionals

Industry experts, tech speakers, and professionals can register to share information about events they're organizing or speaking at. This helps them reach a wider audience and contribute to Bihar's tech community.

Open Source Contribution

BiharHack is proudly an open source project. We believe in the power of community-driven development and welcome contributions from developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts across the globe.

Whether you're a beginner looking to make your first open source contribution or an experienced developer wanting to give back to the community, BiharHack offers opportunities for everyone. You can contribute by:

  • Adding new features and improvements
  • Fixing bugs and issues
  • Improving documentation
  • Suggesting new ideas and enhancements
  • Spreading the word about the platform

Join us in building a platform that empowers Bihar's tech community!
